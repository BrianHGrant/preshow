Meetings and Such

  • General Meetings — First Tuesdays at New Relic

    A mix of formal presentations, lightning talks and group discussions. (You're at one of these right now!)

  • Ruby Lunch — Last Wednesdays at TILT

    Hang out and eat lunch with your fellow Ruby enthusiasts.

  • Ruby Co-Working – Fridays 10am–2pm

    Work and chat with Rubyists at various cafés around Portland. Search Calagator for "ruby co-working" for this week's location.

PDX RailsBridge

RailsBridge workshops are a free and fun way to get started or level up with Rails, Ruby, and other web technologies. Our events focus on increasing diversity in tech, so that people of all backgrounds can feel welcome and comfortable in our industry.

http://railsbridge.org/
